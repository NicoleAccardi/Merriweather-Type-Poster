Merriweather

Sorkin Type

Merriweather was published by Sorkin Type Co, a company owned by Boston type designer, Eben Sorkin. Merriweather was inspired by old book type, but was designed specifically for computers. Its high X-height and thick serifs make it especially easy to read on screens. Interested in downloading Merriweather? Click here.
